# Example: Customer Case Study → Sales Enablement Video

## Context
- **Source material**: Customer success story with adoption metrics
- **Audience**: Prospects in the sales pipeline
- **Duration**: 60 seconds
- **Arc**: Reveal Arc
- **Design system**: Diligent (default)
- **Key message**: Platform X solved a problem the customer had been struggling with for years

## Arc Application

| Scene | Time | Content |
|-------|------|---------|
| The Question | 0:00–0:10 | "What happens when a compliance team gets 3 hours back every week?" |
| The Investigation | 0:10–0:30 | Show the old workflow: manual reviews, email chains, version chaos |
| The Reveal | 0:30–0:48 | The platform in action: automated routing, real-time tracking, one-click reports |
| The Implications | 0:48–0:60 | Customer quote + metric card: "92% reduction in review cycle time" + brand close |

## Key Decisions
- **60 seconds = ~155 words of VO** — extremely tight, every word earns its place
- **Reveal arc chosen** because the "before" state is more relatable than the "after" state for prospects still in the problem
- **Customer quote used at the end** (paraphrased, not verbatim) as the emotional anchor
- **Single metric card** — at 60 seconds, one number is all you get. Make it the best one.
